week 01

April 28 - May 2, 2014

April 28, 2014. This was a very exciting day for me - the first day of my professional career in the field of computer science! Having never been in any type office at which I was actually going to work and contribute to the company, everything was exciting to me. I loved my new office, the stype of renovation, the furniture, my co-workers, and the general atmosphere at Maxxian. I got my own big table, with a dual monitor computer! I've only mainly worked on laptops my entire life and now I get to work on not just one, but two giant screens! During my first week at Maxxian, upon setting up my work station and installing all the necessary programs on my computer, I was introduced to python, their main programming lanuage through a series of fun challenges, found on The Python Challenge. The Python Challenge was extremely entertaining as I was exposed to all the hidden capabilities of Python as a programming language! Near the end of the week, I got to start installing my virtual machine, an image on which I could perform all my own testings, without interfering with any other servers. It was quite a new experience as I never worked on Ubuntu before. To be honest, working through terminal made me feel quite like a pro :)
